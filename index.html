You can have your cake cookie and eat it too!

Should a chocolate chip cookie be chewy or crispy? I say it should be both - crisp crinkly top and chewy center. These Nutty Double Chocolate Chip Cookies are the perfect treat for all the chocolate lovers out there! Easy to make and to customize to your liking.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup finely ground almond flour
  • 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon table salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3 tablespoons sugar or sweetner of your choice
  • 3 tablespoons milk of choice
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cups chopped walnuts

Directions


Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350Â° and position racks in the middle and lower thirds of the oven. In a medium bowl, whisk the flour with the baking soda,cocoa powder and salt.

Step 2: In another bowl, wisk sweetner of choice, coconut oil, milk and vanilla. Mix until smooth.

Step 3: Add the flour into the creamed mixture and fold in the chocolate chips and nuts.

Step 4: Arrange tablespoons of the cookie dough on 2 large baking sheets and bake for about 20 minutes. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheets for 2 minutes before transferring them to wire racks.

Step 5: ENJOY!
